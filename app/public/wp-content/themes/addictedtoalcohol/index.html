WHO WE ARE

Recovering Champions is a comprehensive drug & alcohol treatment program in Massachusetts specializing in substance abuse and co-occurring disorders. To give our clients the best chance of recovery, we offer a structured yet personalized approach that fosters life-changing experiences of hope and freedom. We support each of our clients through a warm, encouraging, and vibrant safe space on beautiful Cape Cod, which helps each member of our community to develop lasting relationships, define long-term goals, and achieve ongoing success.

Our Mission

To help those who suffer from drug and alcohol addiction learn a new way of life, develop meaningful relationships and goals, and maintain the daily process of recovery into the future.

About Us

WHAT WE OFFER

We offer programs to reach clients at any stage of their recovery process, from intensive supervised outpatient care to a less structured return to everyday life. In addition, we provide ongoing individual and family therapy, outreach and aftercare, as needed.

Day Treatment Program

A comprehensive, intense clinical treatment program, personalized for each client’s unique recovery needs in a safe and structured setting.

Intensive Outpatient (IOP)

Daily clinical programming personalized by licensed clinical professionals to assist clients transitioning from PHP care.

Outpatient (OP)

Ongoing continuity and transition for clients moving back into their regular everyday life, all supervised by clinical experts.

Family Counseling

Addiction, recovery, and 12-step counseling by licensed addiction specialist for both individual clients and their families.

Medication Assisted Treatment (MAT)

Medication-assisted treatment (MAT), including opioid treatment programs (OTPs), combines behavioral therapy and medications to treat substance use disorders.

RECOVERING CHAMPIONS?

Family Counseling

Welcoming Community

Personalized Recovery Plans

Psychopharmacology
